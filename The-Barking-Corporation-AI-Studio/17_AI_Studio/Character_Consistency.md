# Character Consistency

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: AI Studio

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Ensure every character looks, behaves, and speaks consistently across all episodes.

---

# Consistency Rules

## Appearance

- Maintain species
- Maintain colors
- Maintain outfit
- Maintain accessories
- Maintain body proportions

---

## Personality

- Keep personality consistent
- Preserve speaking style
- Maintain humor level
- Follow character role

---

## Behaviour

- Actions should match personality
- Expressions should feel natural
- Body language should remain consistent

---

## Voice

- Natural conversational tone
- Consistent speaking style
- Professional workplace language

---

## Environment

- Characters should appear in appropriate locations
- Avoid random placement
- Respect office hierarchy

---

# Never Change

- Character name
- Species
- Outfit
- Primary colors
- Role
- Personality
- Voice style

---

# QA Checklist

✓ Correct appearance

✓ Correct outfit

✓ Correct personality

✓ Correct expressions

✓ Correct actions

✓ Correct dialogue

✓ Character recognizable

---

# Goal

Every episode should make the audience instantly recognize each character without confusion.
