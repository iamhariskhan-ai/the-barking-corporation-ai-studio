# Over the Shoulder

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Shows what a character is viewing while keeping them in frame.

Perfect for coding, presentations, and discussions.

---

# Best Used For

- Coding
- Whiteboard
- Design review
- Laptop screen
- Pair programming
- Meetings

---

# Camera Position

- Behind one character
- Focus on second character or screen
- Shoulder partially visible

---

# Camera Movement

- Static
- Slow push-in

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Over-the-shoulder cinematic shot of an anthropomorphic office employee viewing a computer monitor, realistic workstation, premium corporate office, Pixar-inspired 3D animation.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Blocked monitor
- Shoulder covering subject
- Awkward angle
- Camera shake

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Subject visible

☐ Screen readable

☐ Shoulder naturally framed

☐ Balanced composition
