# Close Up

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Highlights emotions, reactions, and important dialogue.

Focuses on facial expressions.

---

# Best Used For

- Emotional moments
- Thinking
- Reactions
- CEO speeches
- Important announcements

---

# Camera Position

- Head and shoulders
- Eye level
- Face fills most of the frame

---

# Camera Movement

- Static
- Slow cinematic push-in

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Close-up cinematic shot of an expressive anthropomorphic office character, detailed facial animation, natural eye movement, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, premium cinematic quality.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Blurry face
- Extreme zoom
- Cropped eyes
- Camera shake

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Eyes in focus

☐ Expression readable

☐ Smooth framing

☐ High facial detail
