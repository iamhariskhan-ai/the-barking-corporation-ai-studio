# Drone Shot

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Captures a high aerial view of the office building or campus.

Used to establish location and scale.

---

# Best Used For

- Episode opening
- Office exterior
- Company campus
- Parking area
- Scene transition

---

# Camera Position

- High aerial angle
- Wide field of view
- Smooth downward perspective

---

# Camera Movement

- Slow forward
- Slow rise
- Gentle descent
- Smooth cinematic motion

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Cinematic drone shot of a modern corporate office campus, aerial perspective, smooth camera movement, premium office building, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, highly detailed.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Fast flying
- Camera shake
- Extreme altitude
- Sudden movements
- Motion blur

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Stable movement

☐ Clear office visibility

☐ Smooth transition

☐ Cinematic composition
