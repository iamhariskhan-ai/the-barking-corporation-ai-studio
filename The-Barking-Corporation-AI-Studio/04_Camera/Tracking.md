# Tracking Shot

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Follows characters as they move through the office.

Creates dynamic and cinematic movement.

---

# Best Used For

- Walking
- Entering office
- Hallway scenes
- Coffee break
- Moving to meeting room

---

# Camera Position

- Eye level
- Side follow
- Front follow
- Rear follow

---

# Camera Movement

- Smooth tracking
- Constant speed
- Stabilized movement

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Smooth cinematic tracking shot following an anthropomorphic office employee walking through a modern corporate office, professional environment, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, highly detailed.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Shaky camera
- Fast movement
- Sudden zoom
- Jerky motion
- Motion blur

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Smooth camera motion

☐ Subject centered

☐ Stable framing

☐ Natural movement
