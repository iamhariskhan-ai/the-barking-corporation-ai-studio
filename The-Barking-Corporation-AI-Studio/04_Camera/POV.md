# POV Shot

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Shows the scene from the character's point of view.

Creates immersion and audience engagement.

---

# Best Used For

- Looking at code
- Reading emails
- Viewing presentations
- Walking through office
- Exploring environment

---

# Camera Position

- Eye level
- First-person perspective
- Natural head movement

---

# Camera Movement

- Smooth
- Gentle head motion
- Stable walking movement

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

First-person POV inside a modern corporate office, realistic office environment, natural head movement, immersive cinematic perspective, Pixar-inspired 3D animation.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Shaky footage
- Excessive head bobbing
- Fast turning
- Motion sickness
- Blurred vision

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Stable POV

☐ Natural movement

☐ Comfortable viewing

☐ Realistic perspective
