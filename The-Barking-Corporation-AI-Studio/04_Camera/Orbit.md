# Orbit Shot

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Moves the camera smoothly around the subject to create a dynamic cinematic view.

Highlights important characters or moments.

---

# Best Used For

- CEO introduction
- Team celebration
- Product launch
- Character hero shot
- Office showcase

---

# Camera Position

- Eye level
- Circular path around subject
- Subject remains centered

---

# Camera Movement

- Slow 180° orbit
- Slow 360° orbit
- Constant smooth speed

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Smooth cinematic orbit shot around an anthropomorphic office character, premium corporate office, dramatic yet professional presentation, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, highly detailed.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Fast spinning
- Camera shake
- Motion blur
- Subject leaving frame
- Uneven movement

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Subject centered

☐ Smooth orbit

☐ Stable camera

☐ Cinematic movement
