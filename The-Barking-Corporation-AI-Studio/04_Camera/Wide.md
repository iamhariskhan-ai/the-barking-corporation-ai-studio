# Wide Shot

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Introduces the scene and establishes the environment.

Shows multiple characters and their surroundings.

---

# Best Used For

- Office introduction
- Meeting room
- Engineering floor
- Team discussions
- Scene opening
- Scene ending

---

# Camera Position

- Eye level
- Wide field of view
- Entire environment visible
- Characters fully visible

---

# Camera Movement

- Static
- Slow pan
- Slow push-in (optional)

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Wide cinematic shot of a modern corporate office, multiple anthropomorphic employees working together, spacious environment, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, highly detailed, professional office atmosphere.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Extreme zoom
- Cropped characters
- Camera shake
- Distorted perspective

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Entire environment visible

☐ Characters clearly framed

☐ Balanced composition

☐ Stable camera
