# Zoom Shot

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Gradually changes framing to draw attention toward or away from a subject.

---

# Best Used For

- Important dialogue
- Emotional reactions
- Product reveal
- CEO speech
- Key discoveries

---

# Camera Position

- Eye level
- Subject centered
- Stable framing

---

# Camera Movement

- Slow zoom in
- Slow zoom out
- Smooth cinematic speed

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Smooth cinematic zoom toward an expressive anthropomorphic office character, premium corporate environment, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, highly detailed.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Fast zoom
- Snap zoom
- Camera shake
- Distorted perspective

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Smooth zoom

☐ Subject remains sharp

☐ Stable framing

☐ Natural pacing
