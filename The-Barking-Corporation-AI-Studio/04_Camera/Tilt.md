# Tilt Shot

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Camera

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Moves the camera vertically to reveal important subjects or emphasize scale.

---

# Best Used For

- CEO reveal
- Office building
- Whiteboard
- Presentation screen
- Character introduction

---

# Camera Position

- Fixed location
- Vertical camera movement
- Eye level start or finish

---

# Camera Movement

- Slow upward tilt
- Slow downward tilt
- Smooth cinematic motion

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Smooth cinematic tilt shot revealing a modern corporate office and expressive anthropomorphic characters, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, premium office environment.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Fast tilting
- Jerky motion
- Extreme angles
- Camera shake

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Smooth tilt

☐ Subject centered

☐ Stable framing

☐ Cinematic reveal
