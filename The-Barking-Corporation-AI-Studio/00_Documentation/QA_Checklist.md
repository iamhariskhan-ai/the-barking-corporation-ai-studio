# Quality Assurance Checklist

Version: 1.0

Purpose:
Every episode must pass this checklist before publishing.

---

# STORY

☐ One clear story

☐ Strong hook within first 3 seconds

☐ Clear conflict

☐ Logical progression

☐ Satisfying ending

☐ Strong punchline

---

# CHARACTERS

☐ Correct characters used

☐ Personalities remain consistent

☐ Clothing is correct

☐ Character proportions unchanged

☐ Facial expressions match emotions

☐ Eye contact feels natural

☐ Lip sync is acceptable (if dialogue is present)

---

# ENVIRONMENT

☐ Correct office location

☐ Lighting matches scene

☐ Background is clean

☐ Props are correct

☐ No visual artifacts

☐ No floating objects

☐ Consistent branding

---

# CAMERA

☐ Stable camera

☐ Smooth movement

☐ No clipping

☐ Good framing

☐ Characters remain visible

☐ Camera supports storytelling

---

# LIGHTING

☐ Consistent lighting

☐ No flickering

☐ Natural shadows

☐ Proper exposure

☐ No over-saturation

---

# ANIMATION

☐ Natural walking

☐ Natural gestures

☐ Natural blinking

☐ Smooth transitions

☐ Consistent movement speed

☐ No unnatural body deformation

---

# AUDIO

☐ Dialogue is clear

☐ Office ambience present

☐ Correct sound effects

☐ No audio clipping

☐ Balanced volume

☐ Music does not overpower dialogue

---

# DIALOGUE

☐ Easy to understand

☐ Natural pacing

☐ Funny

☐ Matches character personality

☐ No awkward pauses

---

# EDITING

☐ Correct scene order

☐ Smooth cuts

☐ No duplicate scenes

☐ Proper pacing

☐ Runtime between 30–45 seconds

---

# BRANDING

☐ Same animation style

☐ Same visual identity

☐ Same office universe

☐ Family-friendly

☐ Consistent humor
