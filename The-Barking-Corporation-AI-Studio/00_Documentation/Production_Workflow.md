# Success Criteria

Every published episode should:

✔ Be 30–45 seconds

✔ Contain one complete story

✔ Maintain character consistency

✔ Follow studio visual standards

✔ Pass QA checklist

✔ Be optimized for YouTube Shorts

✔ Be reusable as part of the series

---

# Production Philosophy

Build systems, not one-off videos.

Reuse assets whenever possible.

Keep prompts modular.

Continuously improve quality based on analytics.

Consistency is the foundation of a successful animated series.
