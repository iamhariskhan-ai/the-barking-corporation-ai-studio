# Gemini Master Prompt

Generate a high-quality Pixar-inspired 3D animated office scene featuring the specified characters inside the defined environment. Maintain consistent character appearance, expressions, actions, lighting, audio, and camera movement throughout the scene. Ensure smooth cinematic animation, natural dialogue delivery, professional office ambience, expressive body language, and visually appealing composition. Keep continuity consistent with previous scenes while maintaining a family-friendly, humorous, modern corporate atmosphere.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Character inconsistency
- Outfit changes
- Wrong environment
- Camera shake
- Motion blur
- Robotic animation
- Floating objects
- Incorrect proportions
- Low-quality textures
- Poor lip sync
- Unrealistic lighting
- Audio distortion
- Abrupt scene transitions
- Clipping
- Extra limbs
- Deformed faces
- Overexposed lighting
- Empty backgrounds
