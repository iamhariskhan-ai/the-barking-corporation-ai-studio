# Dialogue Block

Purpose

Defines spoken conversation in the scene.

---

Template

Speaker:

Dialogue:

Tone:

Emotion:

Pause:

---

Example

Speaker:
Buddy

Dialogue:
"I've fixed the deployment issue. Let's run the final tests."

Tone:
Confident

Emotion:
Focused

Pause:
Natural conversational pauses

---

Rule

Keep dialogue concise.
Use natural workplace language.
Avoid long monologues in short-form videos.
