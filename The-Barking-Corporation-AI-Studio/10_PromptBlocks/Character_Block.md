# Character Block

Purpose

Defines the primary character(s) appearing in the scene.

---

Template

Character:
- Name:
- Role:
- Expression:
- Action:
- Outfit:
- Personality:
- Position:

---

Example

Character:
- Buddy
- Team Lead
- Focused
- Coding
- Blue Hoodie
- Calm & Confident
- Sitting at workstation

---

Rule

Use a maximum of 3 primary characters per scene.
Keep appearance and personality consistent.
