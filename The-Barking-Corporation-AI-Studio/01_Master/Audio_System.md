# Audio System

Version: 2.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

This document defines the audio standards for every episode.

Audio should create immersion without distracting from dialogue.

The listener should feel like they are inside a modern software company.

---

# Audio Philosophy

Priority Order

1. Dialogue
2. Character Voice
3. Sound Effects
4. Office Ambience
5. Background Music

Dialogue must always be the clearest element.

# Dialogue Standards

Dialogue should be

• Natural
• Professional
• Conversational
• Easy to understand
• Short

Target speaking speed

140–170 words per minute.

Never rush dialogue.

# Silence

Silence is a storytelling tool.

Use brief pauses

Before punchlines

After surprising news

During awkward moments

---

# Spatial Audio

Characters closer to the camera sound slightly louder.

Background sounds remain distant.

Create depth without distraction.

---

# Audio Continuity

Maintain

Room ambience

Voice consistency

Music transitions

Sound effect volume

Do not abruptly remove ambience between scenes.
