# Continuity System

Version: 2.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

The Continuity System ensures every episode, scene, character, environment, object, and animation remains visually and narratively consistent.

Continuity is mandatory.

The audience should feel every episode exists inside one continuous world.

---

# Continuity Philosophy

The audience should never notice continuity.

They should simply believe:

• These characters have worked together for years.

• This office exists every day.

• Every room belongs to the same company.

• Every episode happens naturally within the same universe.

# Character Position Continuity

Characters should maintain logical positions.

Example

Buddy sits on left.

Ben sits on right.

Scene changes.

Buddy remains left.

Ben remains right.

Avoid random position swapping.

---

# Character Motion Continuity

Movement should continue naturally.

# Gemini Veo Continuity Prompt

Continuity Requirements:

Maintain identical character appearance, clothing, body proportions, fur color, accessories, office layout, lighting direction, camera orientation, object positions, emotional progression, dialogue flow, and environment details from the previous scene.
