# Pantry Audio

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Audio

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Creates a relaxed social atmosphere for informal conversations during coffee breaks and lunch.

---

# Mood

- Comfortable
- Friendly
- Relaxing
- Casual

---

# Background Ambience

- Coffee machine
- Spoon stirring
- Cup placement
- Water dispenser
- Microwave beep
- Light laughter
- Soft conversations

---

# Recommended SFX

- Coffee machine
- Coffee pour
- Cup pickup
- Spoon
- Microwave
- Refrigerator

---

# Volume

Background: 25%

Dialogue: 100%

---

# Best Used For

- Coffee Break
- Team Bonding
- Casual Discussion
- Lunch
- Friendly Conversation

---

# Gemini Prompt

Warm office pantry ambience with coffee machine sounds, cup placement, spoon stirring, soft conversations, relaxed workplace environment.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Restaurant ambience
- Crowded cafeteria
- Loud music
- Kitchen chaos
- Children
- Dishes crashing

---
