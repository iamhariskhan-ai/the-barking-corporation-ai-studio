# Office Audio

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Audio

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

The Office Audio profile defines the default soundscape for daily office operations. It creates a professional, productive, and lively workplace without distracting from dialogue.

---

# Mood

- Professional
- Productive
- Calm
- Modern
- Collaborative

---

# Background Ambience

- Soft keyboard typing
- Occasional mouse clicks
- Quiet conversations
- Air conditioning hum
- Laptop fans
- Chair movement
- Distant footsteps
- Light office activity

---

# Recommended SFX

- Slack notifications
- Email notifications
- Keyboard typing
- Mouse clicks
- Printer (occasionally)
- Laptop open/close

---

# Volume

Background: 20–30%

Dialogue: 100%

Notifications: 35–45%

---

# Best Used For

- Coding
- Daily work
- Pair programming
- Customer support
- Design
- QA testing

---

# Gemini Prompt

Modern open office ambience with soft keyboard typing, mouse clicks, subtle conversations, light office activity, air conditioning hum, clean professional workplace audio, realistic corporate environment.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Loud conversations
- Construction noise
- Traffic
- Television
- Music
- Echo
- Children
- Factory sounds

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Dialogue remains clear

☐ Background audio is subtle

☐ Office feels active

☐ No repetitive sounds

☐ No distracting noise
