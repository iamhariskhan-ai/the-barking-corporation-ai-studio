# Meeting Room Audio

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Audio

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

Creates a focused and professional sound environment for meetings, presentations, brainstorming sessions, and client discussions.

---

# Mood

- Focused
- Professional
- Organized
- Collaborative

---

# Background Ambience

- Quiet room tone
- Projector hum
- Laptop typing
- Notebook writing
- Pen clicks
- Chair movement
- Soft page turning

---

# Recommended SFX

- Teams notification
- Presentation clicker
- Whiteboard marker
- Laptop open
- Door open/close

---

# Volume

Background: 10–20%

Dialogue: 100%

---

# Best Used For

- Sprint Planning
- Daily Stand-up
- Client Demo
- CEO Meeting
- HR Discussion
- Design Review

---

# Gemini Prompt

Professional meeting room ambience with subtle projector hum, notebook writing, quiet chair movement, clear dialogue, premium corporate conference room.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Coffee machine
- Loud typing
- Office chatter
- Phone ringing
- Echo
- Reverb

---

# QA Checklist

☐ Dialogue perfectly clear

☐ Minimal background noise

☐ Professional atmosphere

☐ Consistent room ambience
