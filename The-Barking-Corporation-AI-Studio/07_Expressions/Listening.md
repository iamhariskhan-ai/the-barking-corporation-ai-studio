# Listening Expression

## Description
A calm and attentive facial expression showing that a person is carefully hearing, understanding, and engaging with someone.

## Emotion
Attentive, interested, calm, respectful, engaged

## Facial Features
- Focused eyes
- Slight head tilt
- Relaxed eyebrows
- Neutral or gentle smile
- Calm facial posture
- Open and approachable expression

## Usage
- Conversations
- Meetings
- Interviews
- Learning situations
- Customer interactions
- Professional communication
