# Surprised Expression

## Description
A sudden emotional reaction showing shock, amazement, excitement, or unexpected discovery.

## Emotion
Surprised, amazed, shocked, curious, impressed

## Facial Features
- Wide open eyes
- Raised eyebrows
- Slightly open mouth
- Lifted facial muscles
- Alert expression
- Quick reaction posture

## Usage
- Unexpected news
- New discoveries
- Exciting moments
- Reactions to achievements
- Storytelling and entertainment
