# Shocked Expression

## Description
A strong facial reaction showing disbelief, sudden surprise, or being overwhelmed by unexpected information or events.

## Emotion
Shocked, stunned, amazed, speechless, overwhelmed

## Facial Features
- Wide open eyes
- Raised eyebrows
- Open mouth
- Frozen reaction
- Alert facial posture
- Sudden emotional response

## Usage
- Unexpected news
- Breaking information
- Surprising discoveries
- Dramatic reactions
- Storytelling moments
