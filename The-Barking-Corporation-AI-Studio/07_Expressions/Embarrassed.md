# Embarrassed Expression

## Description
A shy or uncomfortable facial expression showing self-consciousness, awkwardness, or a reaction to an unexpected social moment.

## Emotion
Embarrassed, shy, awkward, nervous, self-conscious

## Facial Features
- Slightly lowered gaze
- Soft or nervous smile
- Raised or relaxed eyebrows
- Slightly flushed cheeks (optional)
- Avoiding direct eye contact
- Uncomfortable but gentle expression

## Usage
- Social mistakes
- Awkward situations
- Receiving compliments
- Funny personal moments
- Humble reactions
