# Purpose

Defines the standard laughing animation used during humorous moments, team celebrations, friendly conversations, and light-hearted office interactions.

Laughing should feel genuine, natural, and family-friendly while reflecting each character's unique personality.

---

# Description

Characters react to funny situations with natural laughter, expressive facial movements, relaxed body posture, and subtle physical motion.

The animation should communicate joy without becoming exaggerated or cartoonish.

---

# Body Movement

- Relaxed posture
- Gentle shoulder movement
- Slight upper body bounce
- Natural breathing
- Small lean forward during stronger laughter
- Stable balance

Avoid exaggerated jumping or dramatic body shaking.

---

# Head Movement

- Slight head tilt backward
- Gentle nodding while laughing
- Natural side-to-side movement
- Head returns smoothly to neutral

Avoid rapid or unrealistic head motion.

---

# Hand Movement

Depending on the intensity:

- Hands relaxed by the side
- One hand over the mouth
- Light clap
- Gentle table tap
- Hands on hips while laughing
- Small expressive gestures

Never flail arms wildly.

---

# Eye Direction

- Brief eye closure during genuine laughter
- Maintain eye contact with teammates
- Look toward the person speaking
- Natural blinking

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Evil laughter
- Maniacal laughing
- Excessive body shaking
- Cartoon bouncing
- Falling over
- Mouth clipping
- Frozen face
- Loud screaming
- Unrealistic expressions
- Repetitive looping
