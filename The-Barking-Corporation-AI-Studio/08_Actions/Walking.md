# Purpose

Defines the standard walking animation for all characters in The Barking Corporation universe.

Walking should appear natural, confident, and purposeful while maintaining each character's unique personality.

This action is one of the most frequently used animations and should remain consistent across every episode.

---

# Description

Characters move smoothly through office environments without exaggerated cartoon motion.

Walking should communicate professionalism while preserving each character's personality.

Movement must feel relaxed and believable.

---

# Body Movement

- Upright posture
- Natural torso movement
- Gentle shoulder sway
- Balanced weight transfer
- Smooth stride
- Consistent pace

Avoid exaggerated bouncing.

---

# Head Movement

- Head remains level
- Small natural movement
- Looks in walking direction
- Occasional glance toward nearby characters
- Stable neck movement

---

# Hand Movement

Natural arm swing opposite to leg movement.

When carrying objects:

- Coffee cup
- Laptop
- Notebook
- Tablet

Reduce arm swing naturally.

---

# Eye Direction

Eyes should focus:

- Forward
- Toward destination
- Occasionally at nearby characters
- Briefly toward objects being approached

Avoid constant eye movement.

# Gemini Prompt Module

Professional anthropomorphic office character walking naturally through a modern corporate office, smooth body movement, realistic arm swing, upright posture, natural stride, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, expressive movement, cinematic quality, highly detailed.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Robotic walking
- Stiff body
- Sliding feet
- Floating movement
- Exaggerated bouncing
- Running animation
- Moonwalk effect
- Foot clipping
- Jerky motion
