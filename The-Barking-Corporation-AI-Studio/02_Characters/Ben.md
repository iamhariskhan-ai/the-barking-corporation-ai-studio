# 2. Character Purpose

Ben is the technical expert of The Barking Corporation.

He is responsible for solving difficult engineering problems, reviewing code, guiding developers, and ensuring software quality before releases.

While others may panic during production issues, Ben remains calm and methodical. His dry humor and logical thinking often provide the solution.

Ben is the team's "problem solver."
