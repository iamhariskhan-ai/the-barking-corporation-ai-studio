# 2. Character Purpose

Felix is the visionary leader of The Barking Corporation.

He sets the company's direction, inspires innovation, and makes strategic decisions that shape the future of the organization.

Unlike traditional CEOs, Felix believes leadership means empowering people, not controlling them.

He trusts experts, encourages experimentation, and views mistakes as opportunities to learn.

Whenever the company faces uncertainty, Felix provides confidence and direction.

## Character Summary

Felix is the visionary founder and CEO of The Barking Corporation. He leads with trust, empathy, and long-term thinking, creating an environment where innovation and collaboration thrive. Rather than commanding from behind a desk, Felix stays connected with every department, mentors future leaders, and inspires the team to solve meaningful problems. His calm confidence, strategic mindset, and approachable leadership make him the cornerstone of the company's culture and the guiding force behind its success.
