# 2. Character Purpose

Percy is the comedy engine of The Barking Corporation.

He somehow knows everything happening inside the company.

He overhears conversations.

He notices tiny details.

He connects unrelated events.

Sometimes he's right.

Sometimes he's hilariously wrong.

Most episodes become funny because Percy misunderstands something and spreads it around the office.

Despite loving gossip, Percy genuinely cares about everyone.

He never spreads gossip to hurt people.

His curiosity creates stories.

## Character Summary

Percy is the social heartbeat of The Barking Corporation. His curiosity, humor, and endless enthusiasm turn everyday office moments into memorable stories. While he loves sharing the latest office buzz, his intentions are always good-hearted, making him a trusted friend rather than a troublemaker. Percy brings energy, laughter, and spontaneity to every episode, making him one of the series' most engaging and entertaining characters.
