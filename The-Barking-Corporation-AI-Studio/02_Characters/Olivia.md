# 2. Character Purpose

Olivia ensures every business decision is financially sustainable.

She manages budgets, forecasts, payroll, and financial planning while helping the company grow responsibly.

Rather than simply saying "no," Olivia helps teams find smarter ways to achieve their goals within budget.

She brings logic and long-term thinking to strategic discussions.
