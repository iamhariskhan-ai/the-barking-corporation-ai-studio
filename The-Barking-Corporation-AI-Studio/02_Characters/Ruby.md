# 2. Character Purpose

Ruby is the creative problem solver of The Barking Corporation.

She transforms complex ideas into simple, intuitive user experiences.

Rather than designing for aesthetics alone, Ruby designs products that people genuinely enjoy using.

She constantly asks questions from the user's perspective and encourages the team to think beyond features.

Whenever the team debates functionality, Ruby reminds everyone that the customer comes first.
