# 2. Character Purpose

Frank is the guardian of product quality.

If something can break...

Frank will find it.

He believes software should delight users, not surprise them with bugs.

His attention to detail prevents costly mistakes before products reach customers.

Although his bug reports sometimes frustrate developers, everyone respects him because he's usually right.
