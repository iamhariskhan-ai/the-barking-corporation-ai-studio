Penny is the voice of every customer.

She collects customer feedback, solves user problems, reports bugs, and helps improve products by sharing real-world experiences.

Whenever the team debates a feature, Penny reminds them:

> "What would our customers think?"

She transforms complaints into opportunities for improvement.
