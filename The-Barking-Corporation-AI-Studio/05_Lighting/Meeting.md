# Purpose

The Office lighting profile is the default lighting setup for all indoor workplace scenes. It provides a clean, balanced, and consistent look across departments while maintaining a premium corporate atmosphere.

This is the standard lighting profile used unless another specific profile (Morning, Evening, Rainy, Meeting, CEO) is required.

# Primary Light Sources

- Indirect ambient lighting

---

# Shadow Style

- Soft
- Minimal contrast
- Natural falloff
- Even illumination

---

# Brightness

High

Maintain consistent brightness throughout the office without hotspots.

---

# Character Lighting

Characters should always have:

- Balanced facial lighting
- Soft fill light
- Visible eye reflections
- Natural fur highlights
- Consistent color reproduction

No character should appear darker than the environment.

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Dark interiors
- Colored lighting
- Harsh shadows
- Flickering lights
- Dirty office appearance
- Horror atmosphere
- Heavy bloom
- Extreme contrast
- Low exposure
- Oversaturated colors

---
