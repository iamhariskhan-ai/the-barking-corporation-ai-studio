# CEO Lighting

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Lighting

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

The CEO lighting profile creates a premium executive atmosphere for leadership conversations, company announcements, interviews, strategic planning, and one-on-one discussions.

The office should feel elegant, modern, and inspiring without appearing luxurious or intimidating.

---

# Mood

- Professional
- Premium
- Confident
- Inspirational
- Calm
- Executive

---

# Time of Day

Universal

---

# Color Temperature

4700K (Warm Neutral)

---

# Primary Light Sources

- Large executive windows
- Soft desk lamp
- Premium ceiling lighting
- Ambient fill lighting

---

# Shadow Style

- Soft cinematic shadows
- Medium contrast
- Warm highlights
- Gentle rim lighting

---

# Brightness

Medium to High

Maintain comfortable executive lighting.

---

# Character Lighting

CEO should receive:

- Soft key light
- Warm facial illumination
- Gentle rim light
- Natural eye highlights
- Balanced skin/fur tones

Supporting characters receive equal visibility.

---

# Environment Lighting

Executive office should feature:

- Large windows
- Premium wooden furniture
- Soft reflections
- Indoor plants
- Awards and certificates
- Modern technology

---

# Best Used For

- Leadership Speech
- Company Announcement
- Strategy Discussion
- Performance Review
- Executive Meeting
- Investor Call

---

# Camera Notes

Recommended shots:

- Medium Portrait
- Desk Close-up
- Window Background
- Slow Push-In
- Over-the-Shoulder
- Executive Wide Shot

Camera movement should feel smooth and deliberate.

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Premium executive office with warm professional lighting, soft window daylight, elegant wooden furniture, balanced cinematic illumination, gentle rim lighting, modern corporate environment, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, highly detailed, ultra-consistent.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Dark office
- Luxury mansion appearance
- Harsh shadows
- Dramatic spotlight
- Golden overexposure
- Neon colors
- Heavy contrast
- Cold blue lighting
- Horror mood

---
