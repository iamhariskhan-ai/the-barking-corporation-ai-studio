# Evening Lighting

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Lighting

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

The Evening lighting profile represents the final hours of the workday. It creates a calm, focused, and professional atmosphere suitable for deployments, debugging sessions, executive meetings, and reflective conversations.

The office should feel productive rather than empty.

---

# Mood

- Calm
- Focused
- Professional
- Relaxed
- Determined
- Productive

---

# Time of Day

**5:00 PM – 8:00 PM**

---

# Color Temperature

**4300K (Warm Office LEDs)**

---

# Primary Light Sources

- Warm ceiling LED lights
- Desk lamps
- Computer monitor glow
- Soft sunset light through office windows

---

# Shadow Style

- Soft shadows
- Medium contrast
- Warm highlights
- Smooth light transitions

---

# Brightness

Medium

Maintain comfortable brightness while preserving the evening atmosphere.

---

# Character Lighting

Characters should have:

- Warm facial illumination
- Soft rim lighting
- Monitor reflections on the face (when coding)
- Natural eye highlights
- Consistent fur and clothing colors

Avoid dark or overly dramatic faces.

---

# Environment Lighting

Ideal for:

- Engineering Floor
- CEO Office
- Meeting Room
- DevOps Area
- Design Studio

Office should appear:

- Warm
- Comfortable
- Premium
- Active
- Professional

---

# Best Used For

- Feature Deployment
- Bug Fixing
- Sprint Retrospective
- Client Demo Preparation
- Late Coding Session
- CEO Strategy Meeting
- End-of-Day Review

---

# Camera Notes

Recommended shots:

- Medium Character Shot
- Close-up Coding Shot
- Over-the-Shoulder Laptop View
- Slow Tracking Shot
- Office Establishing Shot

Use monitor glow naturally without overpowering the scene.

# Gemini Prompt Module

> Premium corporate office during evening work hours, warm LED office lighting with subtle monitor glow, soft cinematic shadows, comfortable professional atmosphere, balanced exposure, warm wooden reflections, Pixar-inspired 3D animation, expressive characters, ultra-consistent lighting, highly detailed.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Dark office
- Horror lighting
- Blue neon lighting
- Harsh shadows
- Heavy orange tint
- Flickering lights
- Strong lens flares
- Low visibility
- High contrast
- Oversaturated colors

---

# QA Checklist

Before approving a scene:

- ☐ Warm office atmosphere
- ☐ Characters clearly visible
- ☐ Soft facial lighting
- ☐ Natural monitor glow
- ☐ Comfortable brightness
- ☐ Consistent white balance
- ☐ Smooth shadow transitions
- ☐ Premium corporate look
- ☐ No harsh lighting
- ☐ Lighting continuity maintained
