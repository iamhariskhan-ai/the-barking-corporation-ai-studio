# Rainy Lighting

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Lighting

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

The Rainy lighting profile creates a cozy indoor atmosphere during rainy weather. It is ideal for reflective conversations, debugging sessions, quiet teamwork, and storytelling moments.

The office should feel warm and inviting despite the weather outside.

---

# Mood

- Cozy
- Calm
- Reflective
- Comfortable
- Focused
- Relaxed

---

# Time of Day

Morning

Afternoon

Evening

(Weather dependent)

---

# Color Temperature

4600K

Warm interior lighting with cool exterior daylight.

---

# Primary Light Sources

- Warm office LEDs
- Desk lamps
- Window daylight
- Soft monitor glow

Outside:

- Rain
- Wet reflections
- Overcast sky

---

# Shadow Style

- Soft
- Diffused
- Low contrast
- Gentle reflections

---

# Brightness

Medium

Maintain comfortable indoor visibility.

Avoid making the office appear dark.

---

# Character Lighting

Characters should have:

- Warm facial lighting
- Soft eye highlights
- Gentle window reflections
- Comfortable indoor illumination

Rain should never obscure facial expressions.

---

# Environment Lighting

Visible through windows:

- Rain streaks
- Wet glass
- Soft reflections
- Grey sky
- Water on pavement

Inside remains:

- Bright
- Warm
- Professional

---

# Best Used For

- Debugging Session
- Coffee Conversation
- Team Discussion
- Learning Moment
- Storytelling
- Quiet Coding

---

# Camera Notes

Recommended shots:

- Window Close-up
- Coffee Cup Reflection
- Medium Conversation
- Wide Office View
- Tracking Shot Along Windows

Rain should enhance the atmosphere without distracting from the characters.

---

# Gemini Prompt Module

Modern corporate office during gentle rainfall, warm interior office lighting contrasted with cool overcast daylight outside, soft reflections on windows, cozy professional atmosphere, Pixar-inspired cinematic 3D animation, highly detailed, ultra-consistent.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Thunderstorm disaster
- Flooding
- Dark interiors
- Horror mood
- Heavy fog
- Excessive blue tint
- Lightning flashes
- Wet indoor floors
- Low visibility
