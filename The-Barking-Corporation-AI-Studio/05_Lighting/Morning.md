# Morning Lighting

Version: 1.0

Project: The Barking Corporation

Module: Lighting

Status: Production Ready

---

# Purpose

The Morning lighting profile represents the start of a productive workday. It creates an energetic, welcoming, and optimistic atmosphere that encourages collaboration and positivity.

Use this profile for scenes where the team is starting work, planning the day, or interacting in a relaxed office environment.

---

# Mood

- Fresh
- Bright
- Optimistic
- Professional
- Energetic
- Clean

---

# Time of Day

**7:30 AM – 10:30 AM**

---

# Color Temperature

**5600K (Natural Daylight)**

---

# Primary Light Sources

- Large office windows
- Soft natural sunlight
- Ceiling LED panels
- Ambient bounce light from white walls

---

# Shadow Style

- Soft shadows
- Low contrast
- Natural falloff
- No harsh edges

---

# Brightness

High

Maintain even exposure across the entire office.

Avoid overexposed windows or dark workstations.

---

# Character Lighting

Characters should always have:

- Soft key light on the face
- Gentle fill light
- Natural eye highlights
- Light rim illumination from windows
- Consistent fur and clothing colors

Avoid dramatic facial shadows.

---

# Environment Lighting

Ideal for:

- Engineering Floor
- Design Studio
- HR Department
- Reception
- Pantry
- Meeting Room

Office should appear:

- Bright
- Spacious
- Modern
- Comfortable
- Active

---

# Best Used For

- Daily Stand-up
- Sprint Planning
- Morning Coffee
- Team Introductions
- New Employee Onboarding
- Brainstorming Sessions
- Office Opening Scene

---

# Camera Notes

Recommended shots:

- Wide Establishing Shot
- Medium Team Shot
- Walking Tracking Shot
- Over-the-Shoulder Conversation
- Close-up During Discussion

Natural sunlight should remain consistent throughout the sequence.

---

# Negative Prompt

Avoid:

- Dark office
- Orange lighting
- Blue cinematic tint
- Heavy shadows
- Horror lighting
- Neon colors
- Flickering lights
- Overexposure
- Underexposure
- Strong lens flares
- High contrast
- Foggy interiors

---

# QA Checklist

Before approving a scene, verify:

- ☐ Bright natural daylight
- ☐ Characters clearly visible
- ☐ Soft facial lighting
- ☐ Consistent white balance
- ☐ Natural window reflections
- ☐ Clean office appearance
- ☐ No harsh shadows
- ☐ Smooth lighting continuity
- ☐ Premium corporate atmosphere
- ☐ Pixar-inspired visual quality
