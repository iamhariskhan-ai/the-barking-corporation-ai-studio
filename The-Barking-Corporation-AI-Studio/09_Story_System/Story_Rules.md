# Story Rules

Version: 1.0

Purpose:
Maintain consistency across every episode.

---

# Core Principles

Stories are about teamwork.

Problems are solved collaboratively.

Humor is inclusive.

Everyone is respected.

Technology helps people.

---

# Characters

Keep personalities consistent.

Never change appearance.

Use established catchphrases.

Rotate supporting characters.

Avoid making one character the hero every time.

---

# Comedy

Situational humor only.

Office-related jokes.

No insults.

No humiliation.

No stereotypes.

Family-friendly.

---

# Conflict

Professional disagreement.

Never personal attacks.

No workplace toxicity.

Problems have logical solutions.

---

# Dialogue

Simple.

Natural.

Short sentences.

Easy to understand.

Avoid technical overload.

---

# Visual Rules

One main location.

Clear camera angles.

Recognizable environments.

Consistent lighting.

Smooth animation.

---

# Story Limits

30–45 seconds.

Maximum four speaking characters.

One primary conflict.

One resolution.

Positive ending.

---

# Educational Value

Whenever possible, teach:

Teamwork

Communication

Software development

Problem solving

Customer empathy

Leadership

Continuous learning

---

# Never Include

Violence

Politics

Religion

Adult humor

Offensive language

Unsafe behavior

Personal attacks

---

# Brand Identity

Every episode should leave viewers feeling:

🙂 Entertained

💡 A little smarter

🤝 Inspired by teamwork

🚀 Excited to watch the next episode.
