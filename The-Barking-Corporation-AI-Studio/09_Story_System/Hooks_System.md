# Hooks System

Version: 1.0

Purpose:
Capture viewer attention within the first 3 seconds.

---

# Engineering Hooks

- "Who deployed on Friday?"
- "Production just went down..."
- "Ben... what did you push?"
- "The build failed."

---

# HR Hooks

- "HR just sent an email..."
- "Everyone, please join immediately."
- "Today's policy changed."

---

# CEO Hooks

- "Felix wants everyone in the meeting room."
- "The CEO is walking this way."

---

# Customer Hooks

- "A customer found a serious bug."
- "Support just escalated this."

---

# Funny Hooks

- "Who finished all the coffee?"
- "The Wi-Fi is gone."
- "Percy heard something..."
- "Nobody expected this."

---

# AI Hooks

- "AI wrote this code."
- "Gemini gave us an idea."
- "The chatbot replied WHAT?"

---

# Productivity Hooks

- "Stand-up starts in 30 seconds."
- "Buddy changed the deadline."
- "Sprint planning just got interesting."

---

# Hook Rules

Hook must create curiosity.

Keep under 5 seconds.

Avoid clickbait.

Promise a story.

Deliver on the promise.
